Evac / Transit Status

Impassable roads in parts of southeastern Louisiana: Breakdown here

Monday. Sections of Interstate 10 in the New Orleans area, as well as several I-10 ramps, were shut down. The Lake Pontchartrain Causeway, which connects Metairie, Louisiana, to Lake Pontchartrain’s northern shore, has been closed since late Sunday morning. An alternate route is the I-10 twin span bridge that connects New Orleans to Slidell, Louisiana.
